#PledgeToListen

I pledge to listen to you. Will you listen to me?



I pledge to listen to others who hold different opinions, views or beliefs. I will try to understand their reasons and their perspectives and will respectfully express my own in return.

Listening is the foundation of a healthy society. Join our movement to become a more active listener.

#PledgeToListen is inspired by the documentary Free Trip to Egypt. The film brings people together from completely different backgrounds, religions and political affiliations to see what can happen when people connect at a human level.
